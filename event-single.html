About Event

Cross Nights and Day Cross events are transformative experiences designed to foster personal growth, community building, and future success. These events offer participants a unique opportunity to engage in a variety of activities that challenge both the mind and body, encouraging holistic development.

The importance of these events lies in their ability to create a supportive environment where individuals can push their limits, discover new passions, and develop essential life skills. Through interactive workshops, team-building exercises, and inspirational talks, attendees learn the value of resilience, teamwork, and leadership.

Participating in Cross Nights and Day Cross events can significantly impact your future. The skills and connections gained during these events can open doors to new opportunities, enhance your professional network, and provide a strong foundation for personal and career growth. Whether you're looking to build confidence, improve your problem-solving abilities, or simply connect with like-minded individuals, these events offer invaluable experiences that will benefit you long into the future.

Join us for an unforgettable journey of self-discovery and empowerment. Embrace the challenge, make lasting memories, and prepare yourself for a brighter, more successful future.

Cross Night Event Timetable

Welcome to the Cross Night event! Below is the detailed schedule for the evening's activities, ensuring you stay informed and engaged throughout the night.

1. Class Arrangements

Time: 18:00 - 19:00
Details: The event will kick off with class arrangements. Participants will be organized into their respective groups and provided with an overview of the night's schedule. This time is also allocated for any last-minute announcements or changes.

2. Video Screenings

Duration: At least 4 hours
Details: From 19:00 onwards, a series of educational and entertaining videos will be shown. These videos are curated to provide both enjoyment and insight into various topics related to the event theme. Ensure you have a comfortable seat and enjoy the screening.

3. Midnight Lunch

Time: 00:00
Details: A midnight lunch will be served to keep everyone energized. This break is an excellent opportunity to relax, socialize, and recharge for the remaining activities. A variety of refreshments and meals will be available.

4. Lessons and Activities

Time: After the video screenings
Details: Post-lunch, we will resume with interactive lessons and additional activities. These sessions are designed to be engaging and educational, offering a mix of hands-on learning and discussions.

5. Break

Time: 04:00
Details: A break will be taken at 04:00 to allow participants to rest and refresh. Use this time to stretch, hydrate, and prepare for the final sessions of the night.

6. Recap Session

Time: 06:00
Details: As the event nears its end, a recap session will be held to summarize the key points and experiences from the night. This is a great time for participants to share their insights and feedback.

7. Conclusion

Time: 07:00
Details: The Cross Night event will officially conclude at 07:00. Participants are encouraged to reflect on their experiences and take away valuable knowledge and memories.
